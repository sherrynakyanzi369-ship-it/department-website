Requirement Details
Academic Qualifications Must have completed the required secondary education or equivalent with valid certificates.
Application Form Fully completed application form submitted online or at the Registrar's Office.
Transcripts & Certificates Provide official academic transcripts, certificates, and other supporting documents.
Identification A valid national ID, passport, or birth certificate.
Entrance Test / Interview Attend an entrance test or Interview if required by the program.
Recommendation Letters Some programs may require 1-2 recommendation letters from teachers or employers.
Payment of Application Fees Pay the Ugx 50,000 non-refundable application fee as per the instructions.

Application Process

  1. Obtain application form from the university
  2. Complete and submit with required documents
  3. Attend the entrance interview
  4. Receive admission letter
  5. Complete registration and tuition payment

Tuition Fees

Undergraduate programs:UGX 2,500,000 per semester

Graduate programs: UGX 3,500,000 per semester

Various scholarships and financial aid options available

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: May 31st
  • Interview Period: June 15-30
  • Admission Letters: July 15
  • Registration: August 20-25
  • Semester Start: September 1
